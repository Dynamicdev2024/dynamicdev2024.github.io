Dermatoglyphics Multiple Intelligence Test (DMIT) is a biometric test. It is the study of using fingerprints to map genetic intelligence and traits. Just like the whole body, fingerprints are connected to the working of the brain, and hence a detailed analysis help us understand the innate potential.

The DMIT test reveals our comfort zones or potential zones which help us work towards a stable career.

DMIT Test can be done at any age - toddlers, kids and adults.

Vedic Maths is a collection of methods or sutras to solve numerical computations quickly and faster. It consists of 16 formulae and 13 sub formulae, which can be applied to the solving of problems in arithmetic, algebra ,geometry , calculus, conics, etc.

Jolly Phonics is a fun and child centred approach to teaching literacy through synthetic phonics. With actions for each of the 42 letter sounds, the multi-sensory method is very motivating for children.

The Jolly Phonics approach is widely considered one of the most effective way to teach children how to read and write in English. The benefits of the early introduction to literacy through jolly Phonics include: improved cognitive development, improved confidence and self-esteem.

The findings showed that the teacher had successfully implement the 5 skills in Jolly Phonics, namely
  1. learning the letter sounds
  2. learning letter formation
  3. blending for reading
  4. identifying sounds in words for writing
  5. tricky words

Normal maths are very time consuming and also induce fear in child's mind, where as Vedic Maths is time efficient and a major source of confidence for those who wish to remove their fear of mathematics
